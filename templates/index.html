PRICING MODEL

Pricing for our model will be calculated using three different costs and is represented using the following formula:

Final Cost = Truck + Fragile Items + Labours

Each truck type in kilometres between the origin address and destination address. This cost is represented as the Truck Cost

Truck Cost = Per Kilometre Cost of Truck Distance

For every fragile item an extra Rs100 is charged for the delicate packing and movement of the fragile items. This charge is included as the fragile items cost

Fragile Items Cost = No of Fragile Items x 100

And finally,

An additional Rs 1000 are charged as the labor cost for the packing and unpacking of the goods from the truck.
